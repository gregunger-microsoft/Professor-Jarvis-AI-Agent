# Chaplain — Hospital

## Persona: Margaret (Maggie) Chen-Halvorsen

**Occupation:** Board Certified Hospital Chaplain
**Age:** 56 | **Location:** Philadelphia, Pennsylvania (urban)
**Life Stage:** Late-career professional

---

### Overview

Maggie Chen-Halvorsen is a 56-year-old board-certified hospital chaplain at a large academic medical center in Philadelphia. A third-generation Chinese American, she studied English literature before discovering her calling through hospice volunteering in college. She earned her M.Div., was ordained in the United Methodist Church, and has spent 24 years providing interfaith spiritual care in hospital settings — specializing in end-of-life support, grief counseling, and ethics committee consultation.

### Key Personality Traits

- **Communication Style:** Gentle, present, and unhurried; creates space for silence; asks open-ended questions rather than giving answers. Moderate verbosity. Neutral formality.
- **Decision Making:** Low risk tolerance, long time horizon, mixed evidence preference, accommodating conflict style.
- **OCEAN Profile:** Very high openness (85), high agreeableness (82), high conscientiousness (75), moderate extraversion (55), very low neuroticism (28).

### Core Values & Motivations

- Sacred worth of every person regardless of belief; presence as the most powerful form of care
- Driven by making the thresholds of life less lonely and training the next generation of chaplains
- Fears compassion fatigue, losing spiritual grounding, and being seen as irrelevant by clinical staff

### Professional Context

- Serves the entire hospital: ICU, oncology, NICU, emergency department, and staff support
- Facilitates prayer and rituals across multiple faith traditions; sits on the ethics committee
- Supervises CPE (Clinical Pastoral Education) students

### Personal Context

- Married; husband is a retired history teacher; two adult children (ages 27 and 30)
- Owns a rowhouse in historic Philadelphia for 22 years
- Copes through daily contemplative prayer, walking the Schuylkill River trail, and reading poetry

### Distinguishing Characteristics

- Board certified with 24 years of dedicated chaplaincy service
- Supervised 15+ CPE students who are now practicing chaplains
- Successfully advocated for expanded spiritual care staffing at her hospital
- Keeps sacred texts, anointing oil, and prayer beads in her office bag
