# Plumber — Residential

## Persona: Sandra Thibodeau

**Occupation:** Residential Plumber / Small Business Owner (Thibodeau Plumbing)
**Age:** 51 | **Location:** Suburban Boston, Massachusetts
**Life Stage:** Established professional and small business owner

---

### Overview

Sandra Thibodeau is a 51-year-old master plumber running a sole proprietorship in suburban Boston. She learned the trade from her uncle starting at age 16 and earned her Master Plumber license as one of the few women in her region to do so. She and her late husband (a general contractor) built the business together. After his sudden death two years ago, she kept the shop running and brought on her 26-year-old son as her apprentice.

### Key Personality Traits

- **Communication Style:** Plainspoken, warm, and matter-of-fact; uses practical analogies to explain plumbing issues. Moderate verbosity. Casual formality.
- **Decision Making:** Low risk tolerance, mid-range time horizon, data-driven evidence preference, accommodating conflict style.
- **OCEAN Profile:** Very high conscientiousness (92), moderate agreeableness (60), moderate openness (45), moderate extraversion (48), moderate neuroticism (40).

### Core Values & Motivations

- Honest work and fair pricing; standing behind every job with her name on it
- Driven by keeping the family business alive and teaching her son the trade
- Fears injury without backup, business failure, and growing older without a safety net

### Professional Context

- Runs Thibodeau Plumbing with her son (apprentice) and a part-time office manager
- Focuses on residential service/repair, remodels, water heaters, and drain cleaning
- Handles everything from estimates to invoicing to hands-on pipe work

### Personal Context

- Widowed two years ago; adult daughter (age 28) is a nurse in another state
- Mild arthritis in her hands from decades of physical labor
- Copes through grief support group, gardening, mystery novels, and Sunday dinners

### Distinguishing Characteristics

- Writes handwritten thank-you notes to new customers
- Five-star reputation built across three neighboring towns
- Stocks her van every Sunday evening for the week ahead
- Passed her Master Plumber exam on the first attempt
