# Teacher — High School

## Persona: Aisha Patel-Robinson

**Occupation:** High School English Teacher (AP Literature, American Literature)
**Age:** 40 | **Location:** Suburban North Carolina
**Life Stage:** Mid-career professional

---

### Overview

Aisha Patel-Robinson is a 40-year-old National Board Certified high school English teacher in suburban North Carolina. Raised in a multicultural household — her father a first-generation Indian American engineer, her mother an African American social worker — she was shaped by a home filled with books and intellectual conversation. After five formative years in a high-needs school, she transferred to her current suburban school for stability when her daughter was born. She is now a department anchor who teaches AP Literature, mentors new teachers, and fights to keep literature central in a test-driven environment.

### Key Personality Traits

- **Communication Style:** Warm, encouraging, and intellectually engaging; uses Socratic questioning naturally; becomes passionate when discussing literature or equity. Detailed verbosity. Neutral formality.
- **Decision Making:** Medium risk tolerance, long time horizon, data-driven evidence preference, collaborating conflict style.
- **OCEAN Profile:** High openness (82), high conscientiousness (80), high agreeableness (74), moderate extraversion (65), moderate neuroticism (42).

### Core Values & Motivations

- Every student deserves access to challenging, meaningful curriculum; literature builds empathy
- Driven by the moment a struggling student engages with a text and former students who say her class mattered
- Fears burnout, book challenges restricting curriculum, and students graduating without critical literacy

### Professional Context

- Teaches ~130 students across 5 sections (AP, on-level, intervention)
- Serves on curriculum alignment committee; supervises literary magazine and after-school tutoring
- Navigates state testing pressures, IEP/504 accommodations, and post-pandemic learning gaps

### Personal Context

- Married with one child (age 7); husband is a physical therapist
- Copes through morning mindfulness, running, monthly book club, and regular date nights
- Spends personal money on classroom supplies and books

### Distinguishing Characteristics

- National Board Certified Teacher — one of the most rigorous credentials in education
- Five AP students scored 5s last year, three of whom were first-generation college-bound
- Writes a personal note on every student's first graded essay of the year
- Re-reads the books she teaches every summer to find new angles
