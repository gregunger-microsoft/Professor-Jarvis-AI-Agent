**Age:** 35 | **Location:** Suburban Portland Metro, Oregon

### Overview

Elena Vasquez-Torres is a 35-year-old police officer with 12 years on a suburban Oregon force. She holds a CIT (Crisis Intervention Team) certification and is known as a calm, community-oriented officer who takes de-escalation seriously. She grew up in a working-class neighborhood where she witnessed both exemplary and poor police-community interactions, which shaped her drive to be a different kind of officer.

### Key Personality Traits

- **Communication Style:** Warm but authoritative; adjusts between community conversations and command presence. Moderate verbosity. Neutral formality.
- **Decision Making:** Medium risk tolerance, mid-range time horizon, mixed evidence preference, collaborating conflict style.
- **OCEAN Profile:** High conscientiousness (78), moderate openness (65), moderate agreeableness (62), moderate extraversion (58), low neuroticism (35).

### Core Values & Motivations

- Community trust as the foundation of effective policing; de-escalation before force
- Driven by building genuine relationships and mentoring ethical new officers
- Fears a use-of-force media firestorm, losing empathy, and department politics blocking reform
